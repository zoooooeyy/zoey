During quarter three we were tasked to make and design a preliminary protoype regarding our chosen solution from our ipba. While in quarter one, we discussed the problem, and in quarter 2 we made a solution, now, we are making a preliminary prototype for our solution in order to ensure that we make the most perfect and effective prototype.

Here are the following steps to complete the lifelabs IPBA:
- Observe problem and solution
- Design and make preliminary prototype.
- Make a usability test regarding prototype.
- Get approval to publish prototype.
- Gain respondents
- Preform an audience analysis based on the feedback.
- Make presentation about it.
- Revise your prototype.
- Record presentation.
- Input it on the website.
